A template for a Decision Matrix, a tool for evaluating options against weighted criteria.

## [Decision Matrix Spreadsheet Template](https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1HEJ44ymvbwZCJOrgjyFA7LX3AMO4D4_ii8Uiee2E_xo/edit?usp=sharing){:target="_blank" :rel="noopener noreferrer"}

Make a copy and use it to compare options across the criteria that matter to you.

The technique is described by Rich Hickey (creator of Clojure) in his [Clojure Conj 2023 talk](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=c5QF2HjHLSE&t=2377s){:target="_blank" :rel="noopener noreferrer"}, where he uses it as a structured way to make better decisions.
